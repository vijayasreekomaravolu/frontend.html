Shreyas Iyer was born on 6 December 1994 in Chembur, Mumbai to Santosh Iyer a Tamilian father and his mother Rohini Iyer a Tuluva. His ancestors from Thrissur, Kerala. [4][5][6] He was educated at Don Bosco High School, Matunga[7] and at Ramniranjan Anandilal Podar College of Commerce and Economics, Mumbai.

At the age of 18, Iyer was spotted by coach Pravin Amre at the Shivaji Park Gymkhana. Amre trained him in his early cricketing days.[8] Iyer's teammates at the age group levels used to compare him to Virender Sehwag.[9] During his graduation from Podar College in Mumbai, Iyer helped his college team to lift some trophies.[10]
